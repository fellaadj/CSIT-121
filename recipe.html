Best Chocolate chip cookies

The creator of this Delicious cookie is Me! Fella Adjerid, This is the best cookie you will ever make!

Here are the ingredients needed.

  • butterr
  • white sugar
  • eggs
  • brown sugar
  • baking soda
  • vinilla extract
  • semisweet chocolate
  • water
  • salt
  • flour
  • walnuts
    1. 1 cup of butter, softened
    2. add 1 cup of white sugar
    3. add 1 cup of packed brown sugar
    4. crack 2 eggs
    5. add 2 teaspoons of vinilla extract
    6. add 1 teaspoon of baking soda
    7. add 2 teaspoon of hot water, not boiling
    8. add 1/2 teaspoon of salt
    9. add 3 cups of all purpose flour
    10. add 2 cups of semisweet chocolate chips
    11. add 1 cup of chopped walnuts
    12. mixed throughly in bowl until it is a fully mixed
    13. preheat oven to 350 degree F(175 degrees c)
    14. cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until it smooths, beat in the egg one at a time, then apply the vanilla and stir.
    15. add to batter along with salt, stir flour, chocolate chips, and nuts. drop by large spoonfuls into pan.
    16. Bake for about 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned.
